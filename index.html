About Us

IEEE GTBIT & IIITD Student Branch

IEEE GTBIT and IEEE IIITD are the student branches of IEEE at Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology, and Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology respectively, dedicated to enhancing technical and professional skills among students. Offering networking opportunities, and promoting research and tech enthusiasm, GTBIT and IIIT Delhi are one of the biggest colleges in North India with excellent networking opportunities.

IEEE GTBIT
IEEE IIITD

IEEE Day

IEEE Day is an occasion celebrated every year during the month of October. IEEE IIITD and GTBIT come together this year with 2 physical venues, the respective campuses, to increase their networking opportunities and technical capabilities with an audience of over 5000 college students and a reach all across the colleges of North India.
